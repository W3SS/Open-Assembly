Join Your Movement

Open Assembly allows users to author collaborative ideas called Sparks, vote on those documents, and reach a decision as a group. Groups can operate on pure consensus, majority voting, or perhaps a super-majority. The best ideas will propagate throughout the network of groups and users, allowing the global community to concentrate on the most effective Actions.

A Movement of Individual Actions

We can work together on well-planned initiatives, and act in concert on Sparks generated by the community. By recording your Actions other people can learn and develop them to use at home. By rating our Actions objectively, we can filter the most effective acts that lead us to our desired world.

Open Source and Free

Other corporate websites might filter your content, sell your information to 3rd parties, or promote paid content. We don't roll like that. Open Assembly survives on donations and your ideas push our development forward. We aren't going to sell your information or provide access to repressive regimes.
