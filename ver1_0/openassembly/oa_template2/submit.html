What's a Spark?

Sparks can be whatever you want. They can be an idea, a document, a policy, or maybe a question to the masses. The ideal Spark is something the group can decide is a good idea or not, and has an actionable task the group can work together to accomplish.

Once you've created your Spark you can add images that describe the spark, or link a location if this Spark is local to a geographic area. Others can vote on the Spark and drive it up the rankings. Users can take action on this Spark so if your Spark is action oriented provide the knowledge to accomplish the task.
